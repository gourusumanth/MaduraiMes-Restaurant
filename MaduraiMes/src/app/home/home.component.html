ABOUT US

We Founders of Madurai Mes had an idea to create a restaurant that was more than a place that served great food. We wanted to give locals a place where they'd always feel at home. A place where they could get delicious food at down-to-earth prices, and where they'd be treated right by people who had a passion to serve.

From the moment you step into The MaduraiMes, the experience is an comfortable elegance. Indian paneling and Art Deco chandeliers provide a warm, stately setting for our nationally renowned dishes and acclaimed authentic desserts. With service as gracious as it is attentive, we look forward to impressing you.

We treat people right, and that's what makes new guests feel welcome, and regulars feel right at home.
